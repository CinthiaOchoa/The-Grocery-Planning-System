Prepare the ingredients

Preparation

Cook the rice

Add the rice to a pot with water and cook it according to the recipe timing until the texture becomes soft and ready to serve.

Cooking
3

Cook the chicken

Heat a pan with olive oil, add the chicken breast pieces, then season and cook thoroughly until the meat is no longer pink.

Cooking
4

Add spinach and seasoning

Mix the spinach into the pan with the cooked chicken and stir in the seasoning mix so the vegetables soften slightly.

Cooking
5

Assemble and serve

Place the rice in a bowl, add the cooked chicken and spinach mixture on top, and serve while warm.
